About EKNM Model Polytechnic College

Established in 1997 by the Govt. of Kerala, EK Nayanar memorial model polytechnic collage Kalliasseri is a new generation polytechnic offering cources in emerging technologies. The institution in managed by Institution of Human Resouces Development (IHRD) fully owned and controlled by Govt. of Kerala. Collage is located in NH-11 midway between Kannur and Taliparamba towns. This institution has a proven track record of academic excellence among the other polytechnic collages in Kerala. Admission is fully based on merit and as per the govt. rules. EK Nayanar Memorial Model Polytechnic collage is affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Kerala and approved by Government of Kerala.
